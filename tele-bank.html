Our Telephone Banking Service

We give you access to all your Ahawal Bank Accounts from any phone, be it a mobile, wireless loop or a fixed-line. Start banking through your phone and once you get use to ComTel's telephone convenience; you will be amazed by it

The Essentials

  • Full range of ComTel Services Free of Charge
  • Activation within 24 hours
  • 24 hour access to your account, even on holidays

Telephone Banking offers you 24 hour access to your account, even on holidays from any phone, be it a mobile, wireless loop or a fixed line. And once you get use to its convenience, you'd be hooked on it.

You can check account balance and obtain information on past transactions. You can transfer funds from one Ahawal Bank account to another. Order cheque books and ask for statements. Obtain information on our interests and exchange rates too.

Applying for Telephone Banking

If you do not have the this facility yet, please collect your application form, and hand it over to the Ahawal Bank branch nearest to you. Your Telephone banking facility will be activated within 24 hours.

Benefits of using Telephone Banking

  • You get 24 hour access to your account, even on holidays
  • Check account balance and obtain information on past transactions
  • Transfer funds from one Ahawal Bank account to another
  • Order cheque books and ask for statements
  • Obtain information on our interest and exchange rates

Charges

Just Rs 150/- to set up, and a renewal fee of Rs 100/- annually. After you've got access to Telephone banking, the full range of the services is yours, free of charge.

Terms & Conditions apply Product information and terms & conditions are subject to change from time to time. Therefore, it is advisable to contact the branch nearest to you for the latest information and prevailing terms & conditions.
